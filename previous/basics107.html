LOOK UP

JESUS FILM

Watch one of the following:

  • The JESUS film
  • Life of Jesus (Gospel of John) — also available on Jesus Film Project app (183min)
  • Son of God (170min)
  • The Messiah from The Bible Project YouTube channel (6min)
  • The Passion of the Christ (127min)

These films are an accurate depiction of the life, teaching, and suffering of Jesus. There are three ways you can decide to watch this film:

  • As a historic film: Everything shown becomes a story that happened 2000 years ago and has no relevance for us nowadays.
  • As the salvation of humanity: Jesus died for the sins of the entire human race, but it may not feel personal since you are one in billions.
  • As the price for MY salvation: Watching with the understanding that Jesus suffered for ME personally helps us grasp what really happened. This film is about US—someone suffered the worst torture to free ME from the power of evil and allow me to choose God’s way again.

Decide how much of the movie you will watch together. If you do not have time for the entire movie, consider starting at the one-hour mark. This will still give examples of Jesus’ teaching and miracles before He goes to Jerusalem to suffer and die.
